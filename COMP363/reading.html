Reading material¶

In addition to the required textbook for COMP 363, a number of alternative open educational resources (OER) are available for your consideration:

    1. Lovasz and K. Vesztergombi’s lecture notes on Discrete Mathematics (made available online by L. Lovasz).

    1. Levin’s Discrete Mathematics (distributed under a CC BY SA license).

    1. Aspnes’ Notes on Discrete Mathematics (distributed under a CC BY SA license).

In addition to the material above, this website comprises brief notes on key topics that are covered in COMP 363.
